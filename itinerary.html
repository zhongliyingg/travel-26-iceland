What awaits you

Trip highlights

Nov 13 · Day 2
Reykjavík city
Discover Iceland's capital — the iconic Hallgrímskirkja church, the glittering Harpa concert hall on the harbour, and the charm of the old town. Flat, walkable, and full of character.
Culture Food
Nov 14 · Night tour
Northern Lights
November's long dark nights give some of the best aurora odds of the year. An expert-guided evening bus tour takes you to the clearest skies — with hot chocolate and a re-try guarantee if clouds interfere.
Aurora Adventure
Nov 16 · Day trip
Blue Lagoon
The world-famous milky-blue geothermal spa set against dramatic lava fields. At 38°C, it's deeply relaxing — silica mud masks, swim-up bar, and a steam room. Gentle enough for everyone in the group.
Relaxation Nature
Nov 18 · South Coast
Seljalandsfoss & Skógafoss
Two of Iceland's most dramatic waterfalls, a short drive apart on the South Coast. Walk the wide viewing platform at Skógafoss or admire Seljalandsfoss from every angle on its circuit path.
Nature Adventure
Nov 19 · South Coast
Jökulsárlón Glacier Lagoon
Enormous icebergs drifting silently across a steel-grey lagoon — one of Iceland's most otherworldly sights. Just across the road, Diamond Beach sparkles with ice washed up on black sand.
Nature Adventure
Nov 21 · Golden Circle
Þingvellir National Park
Walk between two tectonic plates in the rift valley where the North American and Eurasian plates pull apart. Also the site of the world's oldest parliament, founded 930 AD. Flat boardwalks — easy for everyone.
Nature History
Nov 21 · Golden Circle
Geysir & Gullfoss
Watch Strokkur geyser erupt 20–30 metres into the air every 5–8 minutes, then marvel at the thundering two-tiered Gullfoss waterfall plunging into a dramatic gorge — especially beautiful in November light.
Nature Adventure
Nov 23 · Frankfurt
Frankfurt Christmas Market
The perfect end to the trip. The market opens on exactly the day you're free — Römerberg square lit up with a 30m Christmas tree, half-timbered houses, Glühwein, marzipan, and the warm scent of roasted chestnuts.
Christmas Food Culture

Waterfalls & black sand beach

花儿与少年5 · 丝路季 · Iceland Reference
Episodes 10–14 · Reykjavík & Höfn · 7-Day Graduation Trip
The cast of Divas Hit the Road 5 (秦海璐, 秦岚, 辛芷蕾, 迪丽热巴, 赵昭仪, 王安宇, 胡先煦) spent 7 days in Iceland as their graduation trip finale. Their route closely matches your family plan, and each day below carries a 📺 note showing what the show did there.

CultureAuroraAdventure
📺
花少5 · Ep 12–13 · Aurora chase — the most moving moment of the trip
On one of the Reykjavík evenings the cast went spontaneously to a hilltop with unobstructed skies to wait for the northern lights. 王安宇 later called it the most moving moment of the entire trip: "Although we didn't see a very obvious aurora, waiting together in the dark on the hilltop — playing, quietly watching — that time was very moving." Director 李超 said: "After 迪丽热巴 was relieved of her guiding duties, they went aurora chasing and played like children — that relaxed ease was the meaning of travel." One viewer noticed 秦海璐 was alone photographing the aurora, until a group member pulled her into the group photo. The production team's live aurora stream drew over 3.3 million viewers. Your Nov 14 evening tour is exactly this experience — the outcome matters less than the shared waiting.

📺
花少5 · Ep 11 · Guide: 王安宇 · City walk, Una's Chinese Museum, lava show & planetarium
王安宇 planned a full Reykjavík day. They walked Laugavegur and the Rainbow Road footbridge, shared waffles on a swing. The cultural highlight was Una's Chinese Museum — an Icelandic grandmother who discovered Tai Chi in the 1970s, first visited China in 1983, and built a private collection of porcelain, Tang clothing and carved wooden beds. The cast brought Chinese gifts and left a collective note: '40 years of Chinese bond — in 1983 you came to China, in 2023 we came to your home.' Wang also arranged a lava show (real molten rock at 1,100°C) and a planetarium aurora show that moved Hu Xianxu to tears. The day ended at a bookshop bar where the whole group sang with a live band.

📺
花少5 · Ep 12 · Guide: 秦岚 · Viking turf hotel near Gullfoss
秦岚 guided a Golden Circle day ending at a remarkable Viking-style turf hotel near Gullfoss — 25 grass-roofed houses built with reclaimed Austrian pine. A repurposed fishing boat served as a sofa, a 19th-century horse carriage as the restaurant counter, and every room had its own basalt geothermal hot tub. Chef Þórarinn Eggertsson cooked exclusively with ingredients from within 20km, changing the menu daily.

花少5 · Ep 13上 · Guide: 秦海璐 · Speed-run waterfalls + Dyrhólaey Lighthouse gales
秦海璐 led an epic day with 5+ stops. At Seljalandsfoss, pre-bought umbrellas snapped open immediately. Hu Xianxu walked under the falls anyway, emerging soaked — a 'walking deterrent sign.' At a rainy stop Qin Hailu called: '5 minutes, speed-run, let's go!' Everyone sprinted shouting 'Charge!' At Dyrhólaey Lighthouse, winds were so fierce walking forward was nearly impossible. Qin Hailu wisely skipped the exposed viewpoint. Xin Zhilei — next day's guide — stayed up until 3am planning meals and the route.

花少5 · Ep 13下 · Guide: 辛芷蕾 · Whale watching, dolphins, sea fishing, hákarl + Jökulsárlón
辛芷蕾 took over (after staying up until 3am planning). The day started with whale watching and sea fishing near Höfn. Dilraba, Wang Anyu and Hu Xianxu reeled in fish; the others joined a crew seafood tasting. Whales never appeared — but dolphins did. Zhao Zhaoyi shouted: 'Dolphin big brother, let's be best friends!' Then a Viking restaurant's hákarl (fermented shark) challenge: Dilraba ate it unflinching; Zhao Zhaoyi nearly gagged but insisted she liked it. Hu Xianxu produced a secret hand-printed photo card gift for each member. Then onward to Jökulsárlón — icebergs in total silence, seals on floating ice.

花少5 · Ep 14上 · Guide: 赵昭仪 · Glacier hike → Category 12 hurricane
赵昭仪 guided the most dramatic day: a glacier hike on Vatnajökull — Europe's largest glacier — with crampons, helmets and ice axes across a vast white expanse. The cast sheltered together through the storm, creating one of the most memorable bonding nights of the entire 28-day trip.

花少5 · Ep 10 · Guide: 迪丽热巴 · Golden Circle (their Day 1) + Ep 14下 · Farewell
Your Nov 21 Golden Circle route exactly retraces the cast's very first day in Iceland (Ep 10, guided by 迪丽热巴). They drove the full circuit: Gullfoss waterfall — one of the world's top-10, glacial water cascading into a dramatic gorge; Strokkur Geyser — erupting 20–40m every 5–10 minutes, the cast lined up creative group shots with each eruption; and Þingvellir National Park — where they ended at Silfra Fissure, snorkelling in near-perfectly transparent glacial water between the North American and Eurasian tectonic plates. 辛芷蕾 said: when you see such magical natural scenery, you won't be bothered by anything again. They finished holding hands underwater for a star-formation group photo. — The season finale (Ep 14下, guided by 胡先煦) brought the cast full circle: a heartfelt group awards ceremony, each member giving a personalised award to another, reflecting on 28 days across three countries. Iceland was called the emotional peak of the trip — the place they felt most free. Season rating: 9.0/10 on Douban — highest in 花儿与少年 franchise history.
